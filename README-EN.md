## PixelFight By Usual24

PixelFight is a 2D pixel-style fighting game. Your goal is to become an ancient mythical being — choose one of the "Pixels" and defeat your enemies by reducing their Heart to zero.

---

### Game Start & Control Key

#### How To Start Game
1.  **Select Pixel**: Player 1 uses A/D keys, and Player 2 uses Left/Right arrow keys to select.
2.  **Game Start**: Press the Enter key after selection.
3.  **Auto Play**: Press the Enter key again during the game to activate the battle mode against a virtual Pixel.

#### Control Key

| Control | Player 1 (L) | Player 2 (R) | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |

### Game Progress & Victory
*   **Victory Condition**: Reduce opponent's HP to 0.
*   **Special Effects**: Burn, Freeze, Stun (input disabled), Invincibility, etc., exist.
*   **Restart**: Press the Enter key to restart after the game ends.
